Saffron

Araliya Beach Resort & Spa

All-Day-Dining Buffet with Live Cooking Facilities

Araliya Resort is inclusive of some of the best restaurants offering you a variety of exquisite dining experiences. Our skilled and passionate chefs, create some of the most creative and decadent food items that are embellished with pure tropical bliss. We pay considerable emphasis on preparing authentic local food items and newer and creative ways to make you relish the region’s speciality – seafood. We also offer our guests a wide array of international food favourites along with fusion dishes that seamlessly blend unique and delectable cuisine to create culinary masterpieces – Enjoy great food and drinks in our restaurants and bars at Araliya Resorts.

Tea Lounge

Araliya Red

Lobby Level Bistro

Embrace the island's deep-rooted passion for tea and life's other pleasures at our Tea Lounge. Indulge in a delightful selection of teas, delectable savouries, and enticing desserts that are available all day, as a talented pianist fills the air with ambient music. At night, the lounge transforms into a captivating whiskey lounge, shifting moods in sync with you.

Start your day with us and enjoy a Sri Lankan breakfast with Ceylon Tea brewed to perfection. Find yourself a table, any time - post breakfast, up to late afternoon and try our scones and pastries, paired with a desired flavour of tea. Brew conversations over cups of tea or just enjoy a cup by yourself, you’ll always leave with a warm smile!
