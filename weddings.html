ARALIYA BEACH RESORT AND SPA

BLISSFUL CELEBRATIONS IN GALLE

Located amidst a romantic and exquisite beach location, Araliya Beach Resort and Spa offers you a host of immaculate wedding venues that even rivals the best wedding halls in Galle! With an exquisite 180 degrees view of the iconic beachfront, easily customisable fixtures and furniture, and well-curated catering service, we make your fairytale wedding dream a dazzling reality! The wedding venues are elaborate and spacious – the largest of it could comfortably accommodate up to 400 people. Also, the choice on offer is numerous – for which you could choose the right plan from a variety of wedding arrangements – the venues are pillar-less and they also feature a dedicated area for dining. Whether it is a private and intimate arrangement with a few of your friends and family or if it is a grand gala event that is embellished in grandeur we will indeed make your wedding day the most memorable day of your life!


Destination Weddings

Exchange vows amidst a lively and atmospheric setting that boasts surreal vistas of turquoise waters, clear blue skies, lush coconut palms, and refreshing sea breeze! Feel the soft golden sands under your feet and the warmth of the tropical sun caressing your skin – feel the joy that brews within your heart and say yes with a joyous smile! This is all but a glimpse of what you would see and experience at a destination wedding curated by us. Amongst exclusive floral and seating arrangements, decadent assortments of food, and live music, there is so much more you could add to host a memorable and dreamlike destination wedding!





ARALIYA RED

PICTURE-PERFECT CELEBRATIONS IN NUWARA ELIYA

Exchange vows amidst immaculate vistas and elegant arrangements here at one of the most premier Nuwara Eliya wedding halls! Our exquisite wedding venues, the Gregory and Victoriya boasts stunning interiors and are amply spaced so that they could comfortably accommodate up to 300 and 350 guests respectively. The ambience at both of the venues is imbued with an air of tranquillity and a luxurious presence – for you could have a ceremonious event embellished in colonial charms. Be it an intimate arrangement with your close friends and relatives, or a grand event with much glitz and glamour, our exquisite wedding venues will surely bring delight to the attendees.
With friendly and hospitable staff, a highly customisable and elegant venue, and a vibrant and delicious array of meals, we offer class-leading services to our patrons and we strive to make their wedding day as the most memorable day of their lives!


Destination Weddings

Being an exclusive destination that is located amidst magnificent mountain ranges, surreal tea hills and misty and romantic climes, The city of Nuwara Eliya, comes in as one of the most ideal and idyllic venues for your wedding. Housing two elegant wedding venues here at the heart of this quaint city, Araliya Red brings you a multitude of unique opportunities for your destination wedding. Equipped with adequate and ideal resources, well-curated catering arrangements and class-leading services we will make your wedding day one of the most memorable joyous days in your life. Reach out to us for which we will be happy to get acquainted and be a part in making your fairy-tale wedding dream a sparkling reality!


Gregory Ballroom

GREGORY Ballroom plays the perfect host to numerous memorable events. Choosing the right venue can make a world of a difference and at the ARALIYA RED we will ensure that you carry precious reminiscences back home.


Victoria Ballroom

A sanctuary of sophistication equipped with state of the art technology and architectural marvel. VICTORIA Ballroom can comfortably accommodation 500 guests and boasts of the biggest ballroom in Nuwara Eliya. Pillar less grandeur awaits to hail and entertain you. HOTEL SUDU ARALIYA

PICTURE PERFECT WEDDINGS IN POLONNARUWA

Your wedding might just be the most special day of your life and it’s a day that you must have played out in your head countless times. So, why not make sure that everything is just as perfect as it should be by celebrating your special day at one of the very best wedding halls in Polonnaruwa, here at the Hotel Sudu Araliya.
A charming space that’s imbued with a comfortable atmosphere with just a sprinkle of magic is what we were going for when designing our wedding hall. If you’ve envisioned your wedding to be an intimate affair – an event only for your closest friends and family – the unique ambience and wondrous décor of our hall will suit you perfectly. On the other hand, if you’re planning for a raucous celebration surrounded by everyone you’ve grown to love, our spacious venue is more than capable of handling it.
Whatever your preference may be, we here at the Hotel Sudu Araliya are all about making your special day extra special – there’s no better choice when it comes to tying the knot here in Polonnaruwa.


A Wedding in Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa is one of the most historically important cities in Sri Lanka – the region has been a part of some of the most significant events that have helped shaped the country. It is fitting then that you imbue Polonnaruwa with a bit of your own history by tying the knot here! Enjoy a celebration with ancient ruins and cultural landmarks in the background and be treated to the wondrous chorus of the resident avifauna as your special day rolls on.
