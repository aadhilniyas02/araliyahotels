Main Pool Bar

Enjoy our Premium service

Relax and unwind at our Main Pool Bar, where you can sip on refreshing cocktails, indulge in delicious snacks, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere. Situated by our magnificent pool, the bar offers a wide selection of beverages, from tropical fruit juices to expertly crafted cocktails. Take a dip in the pool, bask in the sun on a comfortable lounger, and let our friendly bartenders cater to your every need. The Main Pool Bar is the perfect spot to soak up the tropical ambiance and create lasting memories.

Araliya Beach Club Bar

WAVES AND MERRIMENT AT THE UNAWATUNA BAR

Beach Club Consisting of a Main Bar / Pool Bar and Signature Lounge.where the soothing rhythm of the waves meets the vibrant pulse of socializing.
